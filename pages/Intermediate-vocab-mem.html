Vocabulary Memorization

It's time to test your memory!

Try to remember the words mentioned in the useful phrases section, before turning them over.

I´m glad that

Me alegra que

Me alegra que te sientas mejor.

I'm glad you are feeling better.

I appreciate it

Lo aprecio

Siempre eres paciente conmigo, lo aprencio mucho.

You are always patient with me, I appreciate it.

When I have

Cuando tenga

Cuando tenga tiempo libre, descansaré

When I have free time, I will rest

I would love to

Me encantaría

Me encataría poder viajar ahora mismo.

I would love to be able to travel right now.

I wish I could

Ojalá pudiera

Ojalá puediera ir a México contigo.

I wish I could go to Mexico with you.

Since

Ya que

Ya que te verás con Miguel, entregale este regalo por mi.

Since you will be seeing Miguel, please give this to him for me.

Whether or

Ya sea que

Ya sea que ganemos o perdamos, disfrutémoslo.

Whether we win or lose, let´s enjoy it.

To Complain

Quejarse

No me gusta quejarme todo el tiempo.

I don't like to complain all the time.

To Exchange

Intercambiar

Aprendo idiomas para intercambiar culturas.

I learn languages to exchange cultures.
