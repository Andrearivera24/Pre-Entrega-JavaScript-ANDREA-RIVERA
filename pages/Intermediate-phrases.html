Useful Phrases

Hello again!

It's a real pleasure that you are still here, so we have prepared this section for you to continue increasing your vocabulary.
You are probably already starting to have some deeper conversations in Spanish, but they are not necessarily easy to hold. We'll introduce you to some useful subjunctive phrases so you can start expressing feelings, desires, probabilities and even hypotheses in Spanish. You'll also find some uses of "Se" that are not necessarily the typical reflexive ones, and the differences between "Por" and "Para".

How exciting!

What is the most effective way to practice, I hear you ask?

Each phrase is broken down into smaller phrases and words that make it up. In order to put this resource in the most effective way, you can scroll through the images while you listen to the audio. Try to repeat after the audio until you feel you sound as similar to it as possible.
