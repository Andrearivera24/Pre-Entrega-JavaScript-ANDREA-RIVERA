Vocabulary Memorization

It's time to test your memory!

Try to remember the words mentioned in the useful phrases section, before turning them over.

To look good on

Versele

Se te ve muy bien ese color de pelo.

You look great with that hair color.

Being good at

Dársele

A mi amigo se le dan los idiomas.

My friend is good with languages.

Having an opinion

Hacérsele

Se me hace que va a llover esta noche.

I think it's going to rain tonight.

To forget of

Olvidarse de

¡Otra vez se te olvidó mi cumpleaños!.

You forgot my birthday again!.

To break something

Rompérsele

Se me rompió el carro en medio del viaje

My car broke down in the middle of the trip.

To let something fall

Caérsele

Al niño se le cayeron los dientes.

The child's teeth fell out.

To Spill

Regársele

Se me regó el café encima.

I spilled coffee all over myself.

Falling asleep

Dormirse

Ojalá no me duerma durante la película.

I hope I don't fall asleep during the movie.

If I had

Si hubiera

Si me hubiera dormido más temprano, no estaría tan cansada.

If I had gone to sleep earlier, I wouldn't be so tired
